IEC SYMBOLS
IEC (International Electrotechnical Commission) symbols are standardized graphical symbols used to represent electrical and electronic components in diagrams and schematics.
DIGITAL COMPARATOR
A digital comparator is an electronic device that takes two numbers as input in binary form and determines whether one number is greater than, less than or equal to the other number.
