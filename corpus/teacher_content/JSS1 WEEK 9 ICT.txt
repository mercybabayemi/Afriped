BOOTING
Booting is known as starting a computer and putting it into a state of readiness for operation. In other words, it is the process of starting the computer and loading the operating system. When the operating system is loaded, the icons on the desktop in windows displayed.
TYPES OF BOOTING
There are two types of booting
1.Cold booting
2.Warm booting
Cold booting
Cold booting is also known as hard booting. This is the process of turning the computer system ON by pressing the power of the system unit and the monitor.
Warm booting
Warm booting is also known as soft booting. This is the process when the operating system alone is restarted after a system crash or freeze.

STEPS INVOLVED IN SHUTTING DOWN A COMPUTER
Method I
Close all running applications, making sure that any documents you’re currently working on are saved.
Select Start > Shut down Computer.
Click on Shut down
