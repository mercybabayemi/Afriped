TECHNOLOGY OF DIFFERENT AGES
Technology can be defined as the skills, methods and processes people use to improve their lives and environment.
Technology of different ages refer to the various technologies that was available for man to use to solve problems at a point in time of human development.
Technology has evolved through various historical ages, each marked by significant advancements. Seven different Technology ages are:
Stone Age
Bronze Age
Iron Age
Middle Age
Industrial Age
Electronic age
Computer and Information Age

TECHNOLOGY OF DIFFERENT AGES
STONE AGE:
It began about two million years ago. It is called the Stone Age because the most commonly used tools by humans was made of stone. The Stone Age ended when man began to smelt and make metal tool.
BRONZE AGE:
This was the period when most tools and weapons were made of bronze. It occurred between the 1800BC-2000BC.
IRON AGE:
The Iron Age was a time when people around the world learned to use iron for tools and weapons. It was around 1200BC .
