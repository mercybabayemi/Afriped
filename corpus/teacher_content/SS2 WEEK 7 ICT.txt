Produce a Boolean expression from a logic gate.
KEYWORDS: Truth Table, Logic Gates, Boolean expression.
PREVIOUS LEARNING: The students are familiar the two-three input logic gates and truth table, they are also familiar with the symbols.

Interactive board, computer system, Internet
