EVOLUTION OF ICT (TELEVISION AND COMPUTER)
Television (1971): Television combined audio and visual communication, becoming a major medium for mass communication in homes across the world. Invented by Philio Taylor Farnsworth
Computer (1940s-1950s): Charles Babbage invented the modern computer who the world acclaims as the father of computer. The development of computers allowed for the processing and storage of vast amounts of data, transforming information management.

LINK BETWEEN THE COMPUTER AND ICT
As computers evolved, their role in ICT became prominent, especially with the development of the internet in the 1990s.
Computers allowed for digital communication and networking.
It improved the global sharing of information, marking the convergence of traditional communication methods with modern digital technologies.
Computer is programmable just as ICT gadgets are also programmable. All kinds of computers and other ICT gadgets function as one.
